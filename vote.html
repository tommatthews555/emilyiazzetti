Voting By Mail:

To be eligible to vote early by mail in Texas, you must:
  • be 65 years or older
  • be sick or disabled
  • be out of the county on election day and during the period for early voting by personal appearance
  • or be confined in jail, but otherwise eligible

Find out how to vote by mail

The last day to apply for a ballot by mail is Tuesday, April 26, 2022.
The last day to vote by mail is Saturday, May 7, 2022 (Election Day) at 7:00 p.m. (if carrier envelope is not postmarked) or Monday, May 9, 2022 (next business day after Election Day) at 5:00 p.m. (if carrier envelope is postmarked by 7:00 p.m. at the location of the election on Election Day)
