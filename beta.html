Meet Emily Iazzetti

As a current trustee on the Waco ISD school board, Emily is working to improve student outcomes in our community. She values and appreciates WISD teachers and wants our district to be a great place to work.

Emily believes it is important to have parents of current Waco ISD students on the school board. We all have a responsibility to Waco kids. We must work together to continue to improve our schools and the educational opportunities available in our city. The future of our community depends on it.
